About Us

Zulu & Sons Enterprise Pty Ltd is a dynamic, 100% Black-owned civil and construction company headquartered in Queenstown, Eastern Cape, South Africa. Established in 2021, we specialize in modern, efficient construction techniques to deliver exceptional quality and timely project turnarounds. Our commitment is to uplift communities through sustainable development, offering high-quality services across a diverse range of civil and construction sectors.

Our directors hold qualifications in Civil Engineering from respected institutions such as Walter Sisulu University, with additional expertise in Millwright trade certifications. Together, they bring vast experience in construction and infrastructure, ensuring that each project is built to exceed industry standards and regulations.

Our Vision & Mission

Vision: To establish Zulu & Sons Enterprise as a world-class construction company that not only excels in the industry but also empowers and develops previously disadvantaged communities through sustainable projects and job creation.

Mission:

  • Sustainability: Create long-term job opportunities in affected communities and foster a culture of education and growth within the construction industry.
  • Community Impact: Drive positive change by contributing to the economic growth and infrastructural development of the regions we serve.
  • Excellence: Deliver high-quality construction services that align with best industry practices, regulatory standards, and client expectations.
