About Us

Zulu & Sons Enterprise Pty Ltd is a 100% Black-owned civil and construction company headquartered in Queenstown, Eastern Cape, South Africa. Established in 2021, we bring innovation and efficiency to the construction industry, uplifting communities through sustainable development.

Our Services

Road Construction

Specializing in both flexible and rigid pavements.

Building & Renovation

Low-cost housing, commercial, and government buildings.

Stormwater Management

Advanced solutions for urban flooding prevention.

Landscaping & Paving

High-quality stone pitching, landscaping, and paving.

Our Vision & Mission

Vision: To be a world-class construction company empowering communities through sustainable projects.

Mission:

  • Sustainability: Create long-term job opportunities in affected communities
  • Community Impact: Contribute to regional growth and development
  • Excellence: Deliver high-quality services meeting industry standards
